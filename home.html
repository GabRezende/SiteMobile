M O T I V A T I O N

We, youth from the favela, are experiencing a unique opportunity to interact with youth leaders, share our stories, and absorb new strategies to drive our neighborhood forward. This experience will not only broaden our horizons but also develop essential skills in leadership, communication, and problem-solving that have the power to transform our local projects.

Participating in this forum represents a singular opportunity to put our community on the global map. As representatives of Brazil, we have the chance to highlight our perspectives on sustainability, social justice, and community development, showcasing to the world the positive impact we can make.

For us, this trip is much more than just learning; it is an opportunity to feel empowered and inspired to make a significant impact. Upon our return, we will be more prepared and motivated to lead initiatives that benefit our community, inspiring other young people to recognize and explore their transformative potential.
